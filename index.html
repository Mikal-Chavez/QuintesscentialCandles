About QC

Imagine...

Bring the sweet scents of Summer into your home with QuintesScential Candles' assortment of candles and wax melts, handcrafted in Wake Forest, NC. 

Our candles are made with high quality, locally sourced soy and soy blend wax. Sweet Dreams, Peach Cobbler, Island Sunset, Mint Mojito, Summer Sangria, Lavender Chardonnay and Teakwood Linen are just a few of our unique, custom blended, phthalate-free fragrances, designed to take you away from your day to day. 

Planning a wedding, bridal or baby shower and want to wow your guests with a completely unique favor to set the scene? How about keepsake candles with a customized fragrance and color scheme? 

Can't make it out on Saturday mornings to the Wake Forest Farmer's Market? Order online and have our candles shipped to you, or, if you're local, you can opt for pickup, by appointment.


Did you say Mandles?

We're so excited to announce our new candle line called 'Mandles' - candles for men. Lumiere is a blend of sandalwood and amber. Champ is a blend of nag champa with a hint of mulberry. Island Sunset which appeals to both men and women alike is a blend of orange and teakwood linen.
